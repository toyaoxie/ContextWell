ContextWell Lab: Advancing AI, oversight, and well-being as human capacities

A research-led lab developing systems that turn oversight and well-being into scalable, trainable human capacities for real-world AI environments.

What ContextWell Lab is

ContextWell Lab is a research-led space that connects conceptual work on well-being and oversight with lightweight prototypes.

Oversight is not a checkbox. It is a human capacity shaped by context and well-being.

The aim is to make this capacity visible, trainable, and transferable.

Three pillars of ContextWell Lab

Pillar 1

Frameworks

Core models that describe contextual well-being, oversight as a capacity, and how these interact with AI systems.

Pillar 2

Prototype

A scenario-based prototype where users practise oversight and well-being skills in AI-mediated situations.

Pillar 3

Documentation

Whitepapers, diagrams, and references that document the models, design choices, and pilot learnings.

Who this is for

ContextWell Lab is designed for teams and people who sit at the intersection of AI, care, and responsibility.
